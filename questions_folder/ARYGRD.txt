<h3 id="explanation-1" class=" mathjax-support">EXPLANATION:</h3>
<p class=" mathjax-support">If x = 1, then we can at most visit 2 consecutive cells in any row.
Thus (0,0) =&gt; (0,1) =&gt; (1,1) =&gt; (1,2) =&gt; (2,2) =&gt; (2,1) =&gt; (1,1) =&gt; (0,1) =&gt; (0,0)
OR
(0,0) =&gt; (1,0) =&gt; (1,1) =&gt; (2,1) =&gt; (2,2) =&gt; (2,1) =&gt; (1,1) =&gt; (0,1) =&gt; (0,0)
Note that in this case, we cannot go to (1, 2) while returning as it will lead to maximum distance of 3 between (1,0) and (1,2).</p>
